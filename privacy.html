Aether Synth Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 3, 2026

Aether Synth is a music creation app for iOS.

Data Collection

Aether Synth does not require an account and does not collect personal information through a login system.

Microphone

The app may request microphone access when the user chooses to record or sample audio. Audio recording is initiated by the user.

Motion Data

The app may request motion access for spatial or head-tracked audio features. Motion data is used for app functionality.

Files and Audio Content

The app may allow users to import, create, export, or share audio and music-related files. These actions are controlled by the user.

Third-Party AI Services

The App Store version of Aether Synth does not include an active third-party AI composer feature.
